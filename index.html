About

Modern living in the centre
of ancient Athens

HeKo Modern is a two-bedroom apartment in the very heart of Athens — 600 metres from the Cycladic Art Museum and a comfortable walk from the Acropolis, the Temple of Olympian Zeus, and Monastiraki Square. Syntagma Square is 1.2 km away, and the beach is reachable in 8 km.

The apartment features an open-plan kitchen and dining area with warm parquet floors throughout, a fully equipped kitchen with a full-size refrigerator, and a private bathroom with a washing machine. The arched interior layout gives the space a character rarely found in city-centre accommodation.
